Research interest

My general research interest lies in the span of machine learning and geometry. In particular, the widely known "manifold" assumption is that the data has an underlying nonlinear geometric structure, which implies smooth behaviors and determines their properties and characteristics. I try to model this geometry by learning a Riemannian manifold, and then, to develop statistical models accordingly. Also, recently I started to study the geometry of the deep generative models.
